*Textbook: N. Gregory Mankiw, Principles of Economics*

- **Macroeconomic measurement**
  - Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and national income accounting  
  - Price indices (CPI, inflation) and cost of living  
  - Measurement of unemployment and labor force participation  

- **The real economy in the long run**
  - Determinants of output: capital, labor, and technology  
  - Productivity and standards of living  
  - **Loanable funds and real interest rates**
    - Saving, investment, and equilibrium interest rates  
    - Role of financial markets in allocating capital  
    - Effects of government deficits and policies on interest rates  

- **Money and the monetary system** 
  - Functions of money and the structure of the banking system  
  - Role of the Federal Reserve and tools of monetary policy  
  - Money creation, reserves, and the money multiplier  
  - Quantity theory of money and the link between money growth and inflation  

- **Short-run economic fluctuations**
  - Aggregate demand (AD) and aggregate supply (AS) framework  
  - Business cycles and economic shocks  
  - Short-run trade-offs between inflation and unemployment  

- **Macroeconomic policy**
  - Monetary policy: interest rate targeting and stabilization  
  - Fiscal policy: government spending, taxation, and deficits  
  - Policy trade-offs and limitations  

- **Open economy macroeconomics**
  - International trade and capital flows  
  - Exchange rates and their determinants  
  - Interaction between domestic and global markets  

- **Applied macroeconomic analysis**
  - Interpreting macroeconomic data and policy debates  
  - Evaluating responses to recessions and inflation  
  - Connecting theoretical models to real-world economic events
