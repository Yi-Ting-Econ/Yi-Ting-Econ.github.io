**REAL EST 410 — Real Estate Finance**

- **Financial foundations**
  - Time value of money (discounting, compounding, annuities)  
  - Application of financial concepts to real estate assets  

- **Real estate market fundamentals**
  - Property types (residential, office, retail, industrial)  
  - Determinants of housing demand and supply  
  - Location, amenities, and market segmentation  

- **Income and cash flow analysis**
  - Construction of pro forma statements  
  - Rental income, vacancy, and operating expenses  
  - Net Operating Income (NOI) and before/after-tax cash flows  

- **Property valuation**
  - Income approach: discounted cash flow (DCF) and cap rates  
  - Direct capitalization and interpretation of cap rates  
  - Sales comparison approach  
  - Cost approach and replacement cost  

- **Real estate financing**
  - Mortgage structures (fixed-rate, adjustable-rate)  
  - Loan terms, amortization, and debt service  
  - Loan-to-value (LTV) ratios and underwriting constraints  

- **Investment analysis**
  - Net present value (NPV) and internal rate of return (IRR)  
  - Cash-on-cash return and other investment metrics  
  - Risk analysis and sensitivity analysis  

- **Applied underwriting and decision-making**
  - Evaluating investment opportunities  
  - Interactions between financing and property choices  
  - Interpreting real estate investment outcomes
